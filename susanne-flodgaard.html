Susanne Flodgaard

Painter


About the works

The inspiration for my works and paintings I get from many places. It can be from everyday life, nature, from human life, but to a large extent my travels are an everlasting source of inspiration. Experiences from my travels to India, Sri Lanka, Thailand, Morocco and France have made their mark in my works. Inspiration can, for example, be a concrete experience or a thought, a feeling, a touch of a fragrance, a shape and pattern of a sheet, a coloring, a mood, a pattern or embossing on a jar. Often it is a combination in which sensual, emotional and concrete impressions are transformed through my workflow with the images.

About the artist

Susanne Flodgaard was born and raised on the Viborg region. Susanne has lived for a long time in Aarhus, where she has first taught Ergotherapeut and then to Cand.scient.soc. Moving in 1984 to Holstebro, which has since formed the framework for her life and artistic work. Play with creativity and colors have been following Susanne since childhood. Over the years, she has been busy with various creative fields, but for the last 14 years she has chosen to focus on and work seriously with the painting.
