About us

Karoline's House has arisen around the visual artist Ellen Bye Jensen, as a place where she and her network can exhibit their art. The gallery's goal is to spread the art of ordinary people's homes. The ambition is not to sell animal art, but to sell good pictures of artists that may not be so famous.
